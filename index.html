About Us

Fusion Family Medical Practice

At Fusion Family Medical Practice, we are committed to providing personalised, compassionate medical care to ensure your wellbeing.

Founded on 18 November 2015, we have proudly served our community for over a decade. Under the leadership of Dr. Patricia Kashuupulwa, our dedicated team delivers comprehensive healthcare services with expertise and compassion.

We accept all medical aids and cash payments, ensuring accessible healthcare for everyone in Oshakati and surrounding areas.

Our Services

Comprehensive Healthcare Services

We offer a wide range of medical services to cater to all your healthcare needs

Medical Consultations

Face-to-face and online consultations for diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases for all age groups.

Antenatal Care

Comprehensive pregnancy care including sonar, maternal health assessments, and pregnancy education.

Family Planning

Professional family planning services and counseling tailored to your needs.

Immunisations

Childhood immunisations and adult vaccinations to protect you and your family.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care for diabetes, hypertension, asthma, HIV, and other chronic conditions.

HIV Prevention

PrEP & PEP services, HIV testing, and comprehensive STI screening and treatment.

Medical Reports

Medical certificates, fitness assessments, and occupational health services.

Weight Loss Treatment

Slender Wonder program for holistic weight management and fat dissolve treatments.

Wound Care

Professional wound care management and minor procedures.

Specialist Referrals

Infertility consultations, Pap smears, and referrals to medical specialists.

Deliveries

Normal vaginal deliveries and C-sections at both state and private hospitals.

Corporate Wellness

Company wellness programmes and occupational health services for businesses.
