About Us

Fusion Family Medical Practice

At Fusion Family Medical Practice, we are committed to providing personalised, compassionate medical care to ensure your wellbeing.

Founded on 18 November 2015, we have proudly served our community for over a decade. Under the leadership of Dr. Patricia Kashuupulwa, our dedicated team delivers comprehensive healthcare services with expertise and compassion.

We accept all medical aids and cash payments, ensuring accessible healthcare for everyone in Oshakati and surrounding areas.

Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Patricia Kashuupulwa

Founder & Lead Practitioner

Dr. Patricia Kashuupulwa founded Fusion Family Medical Practice on 18 November 2015 with a vision to provide compassionate, accessible healthcare to the Oshakati community and beyond.

With over a decade of experience, Dr. Kashuupulwa specialises in family medicine, antenatal care, chronic disease management, and is an official accredited practitioner of the Slender Wonder Medical Weight Loss Programme.
